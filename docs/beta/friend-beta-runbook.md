# Friend Beta Runbook

> Scope: 5-10 trusted friends validating whether `Closet -> Today -> OOTD feedback` feels useful within the first 5 minutes.

## Invite Criteria

- Invite people who can try the app on mobile and import at least 3-5 real wardrobe items.
- Ask each tester to complete one daily outfit recommendation and one feedback submission.
- Do not pitch Travel, Shop, or Looks as the primary beta promise; they are extension tabs for curious testers.

## Success Criteria

- New testers can complete `Landing -> Closet import -> Today recommendation -> OOTD feedback` without help.
- At least 70% of testers import one item and reach Today.
- At least 50% of testers submit one OOTD feedback score.
- Qualitative feedback identifies specific confusing spots rather than broad "I do not know what to do" reactions.

## Stop / Go Threshold

- Go: most testers complete the loop and feedback clusters around polish, recommendation quality, or desired additions.
- Pause: multiple testers fail before saving the first item, cannot find Today, or cannot submit OOTD feedback.
- Do not expand community, PWA, AR, notifications, or a custom feedback system until the first-run loop clears the go threshold.

## Feedback Collection

- Primary channel: the app's feedback link from Landing, shell, Today, and Closet.
- Ask testers to include what they tried, where they got stuck, and a screenshot if possible.
- Tag feedback manually by surface first: `landing`, `auth`, `closet_import`, `today_recommendation`, `ootd_feedback`, `navigation`.

## Beta Notes To Send Testers

```text
OOTODAY beta is testing one simple loop:
1. Import 3-5 clothes you actually wear.
2. Open Today and look at the outfit suggestions.
3. Record whether one suggestion worked for you.

Travel, Shop, and Looks are available, but this beta is mainly about whether Closet + Today is smooth and useful.
```
